Muhammad Atif Aslam is a Pakistani playback singer and actor. He has recorded numerous chart-topping songs in both Pakistan and India and is known for his vocal belting technique. He predominantly sings in Urdu, Hindi and has also sung in Bengali. Because of his chart-topping and successful songs, he is often regarded as one of the best playback singers of Indian and Pakistani music industry till date.

Atif Aslam was born in a Punjabi Muslim family in Wazirabad (Gujranwala District), Punjab, Pakistan. He began his education at Kimberley Hall School, Lahore. In 1991, he moved to Rawalpindi where he continued his studies in St. Paul's Cambridge School, Satellite Town, Rawalpindi.
